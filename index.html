Guillermo D'Angelo

Geographer & GIS Analyst

I’m a gaeographer and GIS analyst, working in geographic information-related projects since 2010.

Currently I’m working as a GIS Analyst and Head of the Department of Geography of the Ministry of Social Development in Uruguay, and at the University of the Republic as a Research Assistant and Lecturer.

I am qualified in SQL, mainly spatially oriented (PostGIS), and proficient in desktop and command line GIS (Arc and QGIS). I have experience in GIS workflows, the integration of desktop GIS with Python, R and Postgres databases.

These skills are integrated in the management of an enterprise GIS oriented to public social policies.

I’m the head of a team of 15 people, dedicated to GIS, georreferencing, cartography and spatial analysis.
